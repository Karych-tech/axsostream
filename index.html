What is an IPTV Subscription?

Internet Protocol Television (IPTV) is the modern way to watch TV using your internet connection rather than traditional satellite or cable. With an AxoStream IPTV subscription, you gain access to global content instantly. It’s flexible, cost-effective, and offers significantly more variety than local providers.

IPTV Frequently Asked Questions

What is IPTV and how does it work?
IPTV stands for Internet Protocol Television. It delivers TV content over the internet instead of traditional cable or satellite. Our service provides access to thousands of channels, movies, and series through a simple M3U link or Xtream Codes that you can use with compatible apps.
What devices are supported?
Our IPTV service works on Smart TVs (Samsung, LG, Sony), Android devices, iOS devices, tablets, laptops, PCs, Amazon Fire TV Stick, and more. We support all major IPTV players like IPTV Smarters, GSE Smart IPTV, and Perfect Player.
Do you offer 4K and 8K streaming?
Yes! We provide Full HD, 4K, and 8K quality streams depending on your plan and the content availability. Our premium plans include access to the highest quality streams available.
How many devices can I use with one subscription?
Standard plans allow 1 device connection, while Premium plans support up to 3 simultaneous connections. You can watch on multiple devices but only stream on the allowed number at the same time.
Is there a free trial available?
We offer a 24-hour free trial for new customers to test our service. Contact us via WhatsApp to get started with your trial period.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept payments via PayPal, credit/debit cards, and cryptocurrency. All payments are processed securely, and you'll receive your login details immediately after payment confirmation.
Do you provide 24/7 customer support?
Yes, our support team is available 24/7 via WhatsApp, email, and live chat. We respond quickly to technical issues and provide setup assistance for all devices.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no long-term contracts or hidden fees. Your service will remain active until the end of your paid period.
